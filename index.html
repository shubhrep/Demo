About

What is Planet-M

Planet-M Digital Media is an Indian digital entertainment company, headquartered in Mumbai. The company was first launched in 1999 by Lashit Sanghvi, Rakesh Sharma Jhunjhunwala, and Neeraj Roy as an online promotions agency. The company has since grown to also serve as an aggregator, developer, publisher, and distributor of Bollywood and Asian entertainment. The company first launched in 1999 as "Planet-M", a promotional marketing portal. In 2000 the company acquired Indiafm and in the following years began to work marketing campaigns for companies such as Coca-Cola and Nike. In 2007 planet-M launched their Music portal and in 2009, the company re-launched their website and company name, changing it to Planet-M Media Entertainment. In 2012 Planet-M Media Entertainment launched Artist aloud!, a digital platform for artists and music fans.[ It won a gold award for Best Digital Communications Campaign from the Promotion Marketing Awards of Asia (2009).
